Pixxels Recipes is a Mod to Add different Recipes for Vanilla Items, like the Tinkerers Workshop,

Currently Adds:

- 1 Custom Item
 - An "Minion Counter" which shows your amount of Minion Slots and summoned Minions (But not Sentries ATM)
 
- 5 Recipes
 - Tinkers Workshop
 - Slime Staff
 - Wrench (Red only atm)
 - Wire
 - Hellforge (inspired by the Adamantite/Titanium one)
